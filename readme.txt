== Description ==

Integrate your favourite session recording and analytics tool with ease. This plugin allows you to integrate the most popular services: Smartlook, Fullstory, Hotjar and  Mouseflow. **Easy integration – just put the tracking ID and you are ready to go.**

Some of the key features that those services include:

* **Real-Time Data**: Real-time data that allows you to track user behavior as it happens on your website. This feature lets you see how many users are currently on your site, what pages they are viewing, and where they are coming from.

* **Audience Reports**: Detailed audience reports that give you insights into the demographics, interests, and behaviors of your website visitors. This data helps you understand your audience and tailor your marketing efforts to their needs.

* **Behavior Reports**: Behavior reports that show how users are interacting with your website. This feature helps you identify which pages are most popular, how long users are staying on your site, and which pages are leading to conversions.

* **Session Replay**: Detailed recordings of every user interaction on your website or mobile app, including clicks, scrolls, and pageviews.

* **Heatmaps**: Visual representations of user engagement on your website, highlighting which areas are receiving the most attention.

* **Conversion Funnels**: A powerful tool for analysing and optimising the user journey from start to finish, identifying potential areas for improvement.

* **Search and Segmentation**: A powerful search engine that allows businesses to filter sessions based on specific criteria, such as user location, device type, and behaviour.

* **Rage Clicks and Error Tracking**: Identifying and resolving issues on your website that cause frustration for users, such as broken links or error messages.

* **Integrations**: Integrate with a range of popular marketing and analytics tools, including Google Analytics, Slack, and Salesforce.

* **Customizable Dashboards**: Customizable dashboards that allow businesses to track and visualise key metrics, such as conversion rates and user engagement.

* **Collaboration Tools**: Provide a range of collaboration tools, including the ability to share sessions and notes with team members, making it easier for businesses to collaborate and solve problems quickly.

* **Privacy and Security**: Privacy and security is taken seriously, offering features such as GDPR compliance, two-factor authentication, and encryption to keep your data safe and secure.

* **Custom Events**: Create custom events that track specific user actions, such as button clicks or form submissions, providing more detailed insights into user behaviour.

* **Form Analytics**: Detailed analytics on form usage and submission, including field completion rates and drop-off points, helping businesses identify areas for improvement in their forms.

* And much more…

== Installation ==

1. Upload `analytics-integrator` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Go to Settings / Analytics Integrator and enter your tracking code of the service you want to integrate
